Worlds

In ClassLigte you will be met with a custom world system where every 1000 tiles a new world is placed in both directions, so in total that would be 2000 tiles per world.

World 1

World 1 is the starter world. Here you can roam around the plain with little threats. You start with 3 skills in this world, but nothing too complicated.

Skills

In world 1 you have a total of 3 skills.
These skills are fighting, fishing and collecting.
In world 1 you fight rats for the fighting skill.
For the fishing skill you get normal water. In here you can find fish, but also junk which can be used for crafts!
For the collecting skill you can right-click grass. Collecting gives important items for progression such as early game iron and ropes.

Enemies

In world 1 there only is 1 enemy. That is the rat. The rat is a weak enemy that can drop bones and rat teeth upon death.

Boss

The boss for world 1 is the rootlord. The rootlord has several attacks, but must be defeated to get to world 2.
The attacks of the rootlord are the root attack, the vine slash and the acorn attack.
With the root attack the rootlord summons roots at the bottom of the world and if you step on those roots you will get stuck.
The rootlord used one of it's vines as a whip to deal a lot of damage with the vine slash.
With the acorn attack the rootlord shoots a bunch of acorns high in the sky. After a few seconds they come back at high speed dealing a lot of damage.
